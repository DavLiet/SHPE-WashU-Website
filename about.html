About

Started in 2016, the SHPE chapter of Washington University seeks to empower the hispanic student community within the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, while contributing to the Saint Louis community. Events include workshops provided by the Career Center, inviting alumni and professionals in the area to speak to our members, and even networking events ranging from small to large and culminating with the National SHPE Conference. We invite all undergraduate WashU students to come to our events. We help the community with events such as Noche de Ciencias, where we join the St. Louis community in encouraging underprivileged middle- and high-school students to understand the fundamentality of engineering and sciences in college.
